Displays the active url (or any selected link or text) as a qrcode for easy sharing between devices with a qrcode scanner like mobile/smart phones and tablets. Also allows saving and copying to the clipboard pasting in other programms. Usages:
  • Left click the toolbar button/icon and in the popup just type into the Edit textbox
  • Right click on any selected text, link or image and then click on the "Show QRCode" content menu item
